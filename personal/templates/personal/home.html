Receipy

Is an application designed to make businesses in the restaurant industry better equipped to systematize their products to drive down cost.

Receipy comes with functionality that allows businesses to get informed at the right time when something has a huge change in cost. The dashboard serves to visualize the most vital changes in your data, so you can be equipped to take the right actions.
