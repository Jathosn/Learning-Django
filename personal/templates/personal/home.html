Receipy

Is an application designed to make businesses in the restaurant industry better equipped to systematize their recipes to drive down cost.

Receipy comes with functionality that allows businesses to get informed when something has a huge change in cost, both high and low. The dashboard serves to visualize the most vital changes in your data, so you can be equipped to take the right actions.
